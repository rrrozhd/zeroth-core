### Integration Gaps

1. **HIGH - Memory resolver not wired (INT-01)** [MEM-01 through MEM-06]
   - bootstrap.py creates registry and populates it, but never creates MemoryConnectorResolver or injects into AgentRunner
   - All memory reads/writes silently no-op at runtime
   - Fix: Create MemoryConnectorResolver(registry=memory_registry) and inject into AgentRunner

2. **HIGH - BudgetEnforcer not injected (INT-02)** [ECON-03]
   - bootstrap.py creates BudgetEnforcer but never injects into AgentRunner instances
   - Pre-execution budget checking will never fire
   - Fix: Inject budget_enforcer into AgentRunner at construction or dispatch time

3. **LOW - Regulus health check broken (INT-03)** [OPS-01]
   - RegulusClient doesn't expose base_url attribute; health probe always reports unavailable
   - Fix: Add self.base_url = base_url in RegulusClient.__init__
