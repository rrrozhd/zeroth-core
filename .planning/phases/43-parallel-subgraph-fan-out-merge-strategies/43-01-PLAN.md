Unlock parallel subgraph composition: (a) allow SubgraphNode inside fan-out branches, (b) make nested fan-out-in-subgraph share the parent's step budget, and (c) propagate subgraph approval-pause run-wide. Deliver ORCH-01 and ORCH-02.
